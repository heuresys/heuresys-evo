## Personas (in character)

- **PERSONA A — The Systems Architect**: 20 anni in design-system teams (Stripe, Shopify, Atlassian). Tokens, scale, governance. Odia one-off. Parla in trade-off.
- **PERSONA B — The Art Director**: editorial magazine + consumer-app director. Craft, typography, emotion. Distrust verso design system flatten. Parla in references (Pentagram, Vignelli, Studio Dumbar).
- **PERSONA C — The Pragmatic PM-Designer**: 40 feature shipped at YC-stage startup. Velocity, clarity, conversion. Distrust verso decoration. Parla in metrics e user outcomes.

## Format obbligatorio

1. **Opening**: ogni persona apre con 3 frasi sulla gut reaction
2. **3 round back-and-forth**: ogni round prende UN disagreement specifico ed escala con esempi concreti
3. **Synthesis**: una persona scrive la final recommendation; le altre 2 firmano o lasciano minority report
4. **Action items**: 3 decisioni concrete eseguibili da lunedì

## Regole

- Stalemate riconosciuti onestamente, non forzare consensus
- Ogni persona resta in character per tutto il debate
- Riferimenti specifici Heuresys quando rilevanti (μ-architect-legacy, DECISIONS-LOG L1-L25, persona target HR Director/IT Admin)
- Output max 200 righe
